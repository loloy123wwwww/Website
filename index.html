after that i waited to get shipping number and money for soldering iron, once i did i bought it and waited

after a month+ my soldering iron arrived but the keys for macropad and micro controller didn't (these were shipped from america)

i decided to call the shipping company and they told me they lost it, so i lost hope

then i tried to make a keyboard project which also judges needed a lot of stuff changed on it, so it isn't finished yet

Campfire

i signed up for campfire happening in prague 27.2-1.3 2026

i got fast reply that i can come and what to take with me, i invited my friend which came there with me

when we got there it was amazing, a lot of food, cool people and we even found third teammate

it was my first time making game in godot but we somehow managed to get first place

if you would like to play the game heres the link to github github.com/caves_game and heres a link for itch.io (playable in browser) itch.io/caves_game

overall it was amazing and i won a shark plushie (i recommend irl events for everyone to learn, win prizes and find friends)
